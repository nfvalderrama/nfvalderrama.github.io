About Me

About me

I am a Biomedical Engineer from Universidad de Los Andes. Currently, I am pursuing my M.Sc. in Biomedical Engineering with an emphasis in Artificial Intelligence at Universidad de Los Andes. Also, I am a teaching assistant and a researcher at the Center for Research and Formation in Artificial Intelligences under the supervision of Prof. Pablo Arbeláez.

My main interests include molecular biology, genetics, nature conservation, and how to attend these problems with the aid of Machine Learning, Deep Learning, and Computer Vision. I also work with computer vision in other applications, such as Endoscopic Surgical Video Analysis and Petrography.

I like singing and manual activities in my free time, such as ceramics, painting, embroidery, and healthy cooking (specially desserts and pancakes).
